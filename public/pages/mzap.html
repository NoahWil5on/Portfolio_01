mZAP

mZAP is an iOS, Android, and web app built with Ionic, Typescript, NodeJS, and Firebase. The apps function is to combat the spread of Zika in remote regions of the world by allowing users to report the location of environmental risks (standing water, mosquito breeding grounds, abandoned buildings etc.) and communicate to resolve the issues around their community.
