About Me

Hi! By now you probably know my name is Noah Wilson. I'm also a 4th year student at Rochester Institute of Technology and well on my way to graduate this spring with a BS in Game Desgin and Development!

During that time I've been involved with a ton of cool projects like publishing a mobile app that's helping to stop the spread of the Zika virus, I've implemented a content management system that allows inexperienced clients to take control of their own website, and of course games. Lots of games!

I have a fair amount of experience with object oriented programming in C#, Java, and C++ as well as a diverse background with web technologies like JavaScript, NodeJS, Angular 2 and Firebase to name a few.
